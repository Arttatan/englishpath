Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

EnglishPath (“we”, “us”) provides an English-learning website with lessons, exercises and optional account features. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Who we are

The site is operated by the EnglishPath project. For privacy questions, contact us via Contact or email art.tatan@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email address and authentication details when you sign up (handled by Supabase Auth).
  • Learning progress: lesson scores and completion dates if you are logged in.
  • Technical data: basic server/access logs, cookie preferences stored in your browser.
  • Payment data (future): if you buy Premium via Stripe, payment details are processed by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers.

3. How we use data

  • To provide and improve lessons, exercises and your account.
  • To keep you signed in and secure the service.
  • To send essential emails (confirmation, password reset).
  • To show advertising (e.g. Google AdSense) on free content, if enabled — and to respect your cookie choice where possible.
  • To manage Premium subscriptions when Stripe is connected.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies / local storage for login sessions and your cookie preference. We may use analytics or advertising cookies (for example Google AdSense) after you accept them via the cookie banner. You can choose “Essential only” to limit non-essential cookies in our banner (third-party tools may still have their own controls).

5. Service providers

  • Supabase — authentication and database.
  • Vercel — website hosting.
  • Stripe (planned) — payments.
  • Google (planned) — AdSense / analytics.

6. Data retention

We keep account and progress data while your account exists. You may request deletion by contacting us. Auth emails and security logs may be retained for a limited time for fraud prevention.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. EU/EEA under GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete or export your data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Children

The site is for general learning. If you are under the digital consent age in your country, please use the site with a parent or guardian.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
